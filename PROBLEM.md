# Hospital Management System (HMS)

The Hospital Management System (HMS) is a platform designed to digitalize and streamline core healthcare operations, transitioning from a centralized monolith to a modern, event-driven microservices architecture. The system facilitates patient onboarding, doctor scheduling, real-time appointment booking, digital prescription writing, invoice generation, and insurance claim processing. It is designed to be used by five distinct roles: Patients (who book appointments and view prescriptions), Doctors (who manage schedules and write prescriptions), Receptionists/Staff (who manage patient details and schedule appointments), Insurance Agents (who review and process claims), and Administrators (who oversee system configuration, roles, and service health).
