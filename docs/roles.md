# User Roles and Responsibilities

Here are the 5 core roles in the Hospital Management System and their primary capabilities:

1. **Patient**: Can register an account, search for doctors, book and cancel appointments, and view their digital prescriptions.
2. **Doctor**: Can update their availability schedules, view their appointments, and issue digital prescriptions to patients.
3. **Receptionist**: Can create and update patient profiles, book appointments on behalf of patients, and manage doctor calendars.
4. **Insurance Agent**: Can view medical claims generated by patient appointments, verify insurance policy status, and approve or reject claims.
5. **Administrator**: Can manage system-wide settings, configure role permissions, monitor service health, and view system audit logs.
